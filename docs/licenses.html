Third-Party Data Services

NOAA Aviation Weather Center

aviationweather.gov

NOAA provides public aviation weather data including METARs, TAFs, SIGMETs, PIREPs, and G-AIRMETs. This data is produced by the U.S. National Weather Service and is in the public domain.

Open-Meteo

open-meteo.com

Open-Meteo provides free weather forecast APIs. Used for upper-atmosphere wind data along flight routes. Licensed under CC BY 4.0 for non-commercial use.

AeroDataBox

aerodatabox.com
